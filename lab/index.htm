[closed] Rating Perceptual Quality of videos

Lead researcher: Zihao Qi (zihao.qi@bristol.ac.uk)

Period: Closed

Subjects needed: Closed

Reward: Closed

Participant Infomation: [Link]

You are invited to participate in a subjective study for video deep learning researches.

In the subjective test, you will be watching a series of short videos and rating their perceptual quality. Your responses will help to facilitate the researches in video compression and objective video quality assessment.

You may participate in this test either offline or online using Amazon MTurk Worker Sandbox.

[in-action] Data Collection for Developing AI That Understands Struggle

Lead researcher: Shijia Feng (shijia.feng.2019@bristol.ac.uk)

Period: 15 Apr ~ 31 Apr (or reach the number of subjects needed)

Subjects needed: 80

Reward: £5 Love2shop reward code

Participant Infomation: [Link]

We are carrying out second stage data collection for developing AI that is able to understand struggles. No matter whether you are an undergraduate student or a PGR student, we warmly welcome you to participate in our research study! This is a valuable opportunity to contribute to cutting-edge research in AI and make a difference in how people learn.

Who we are:

We are researchers from the VI-Lab in the School of Computer Science at the University of Bristol. We are studying how people learn and interact with the world around them and how to build AI models to help. We are especially interested in understanding how to identify when someone is struggling with a task, which is crucial for building intelligent support systems.

We are inviting you to participate in our research study by:
  • Recording yourself performing various tasks like knot tying, Origami (folding paper), or using chopsticks (using a safe, head-mounted camera)
  • The process is simple, takes approximately 30 minutes, and involves minimal risk.
  • Your participation will significantly contribute to this exciting research!
Benefits
  • We will send you £5 Love2shop reward code as a reward for your participation.
  • You'll be at the forefront of AI development!
  • You'll contribute to research that can help people learn more effectively.
